Who we are

Aubergine & Company is focused on making healthy eating easy, convenient, and sustainable. We offer food that is nutritious and delicious. We offer natural, fresh house-made salads, sandwiches, bowls, soups, acai bowls, juices, smoothies and desserts without any preservatives or refined sugar.
